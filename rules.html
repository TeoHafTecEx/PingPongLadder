Quick Summary (Read This First)

Movement depends on whether you challenged up or did a push-down.

A) If you challenge UP (you‚Äôre below them)

House Rules (Keep It Simple)

Serves: Serves must be cross-court.

Net rules: Net and over = re-serve. Net and off the table = you lose the point.

Takeovers: You are allowed two takeovers; on your third, the serve must be legal or you lose the point.
